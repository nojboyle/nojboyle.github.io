Welcome to Season Shifter!

Your one stop place for all of your seasonal escapes!

Objectives:

    Explore the world and collect coins!

    Autumn: Collect 10 coins hidden in the world to shift seasons over to Winter!

    Winter: Coins will fall from the sky like snow, fading in and out of view. Collect 15 of them to shift seasons over to Spring!

    Spring: Coins will move on the breeze like pollen in the wind. Catch and collect 10 of them to shift seasons over to Summer!

    Summer: Coins will move around like waves on water; nigh uncatchable and stopping for nothing. Collect 20 of them and you will win the game!

    Unlock all of the seasons to restore seasonal balance to the universe!
